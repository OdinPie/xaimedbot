Clinical Diagnostic Companion

A diagnostic conversation engine that extracts key clinical features from your symptoms and reveals its reasoning through matched-symptom scoring. It uses doctor-like follow-up questioning and a two-stage process consisting of symptom evaluation followed by test-based verification to converge on the most plausible explanations.
